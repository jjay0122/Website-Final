My name is Justin Yap and I am a bisexual Filipino-American, my pronouns are he/him/his. I was borned and raised in Stockton California, but I am currently located in San Jose, California.

Art and film are two of my biggest passions. I would love to create art pieces and films that inspire others, connect with others, and express many feelings from the struggles to the best moments.

My art pieces would generally focus on fantasy and fictional aspects that would each have its own deep meaning. My films would generally focus on giving more and better representation to the LGBTQ+ community and the Asian-American community. My future art pieces and films will include these components but will not be limited to them.

Some of my biggest inspirations in art and film are Pablo Picasso, Stan Lee, and Tim Burton. Pablo Picasso was one of the first artists that i drew an interest to and he was one of the reasons why I wanted to pursue art. Stan Lee created so many characters that it inspired me to want to create some of my own. Tim Burton is known for dark-like movies and his works will defiently inspire some of my future films.
