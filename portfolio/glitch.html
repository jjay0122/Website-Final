Through the use of the Sublime text editor, I edited two photos. The first photo is a photo of one of the beaches in Boracay, Philippines, and the second is a photo of a street and buildings in Tokyo, Japan. I chose the photo of Boracay, Philippines because it’s one of the places I used to visit when I visit my family in the Philippines. Then, I chose the photo of Tokyo, Japan because it’s one of many places I want to visit in the future. Additionally, I chose these two photos to represent my past, Boracay, and my future, Tokyo. The glitches don’t change the meanings of the images but enforce my concept. The glitches of the Boracay images are full of colors that pop because in the past I used to use a lot of color in my art. On the other hand, the glitches of the Tokyo images have less color and more of a dark feel because now my art is more focused on concepts and storytelling than aspects like color.
